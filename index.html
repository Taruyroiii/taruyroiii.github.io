Super Furious Lucky Block Data Pack
A passion project of mine, which is a data pack for Minecraft 1.19.x and is one of the best lucky block data packs that are currently available on recent Minecraft versions. Due to recent frequent changes in data packs in Minecraft 1.20.6 and 1.21, it will eventually be updated.
Visit PlanetMinecraft Page
Random Village and Villager Names Data Pack
A data pack that tested my ability to randomize vowels and consonants to make "believable-sounding" names (while also integrating real-world name combinations). In vanilla Minecraft, villagers and villages lack names. As a fan of role-playing games, I made this data pack to improve integrating yourself to the Minecraft world as you can encounter more than a billion villagers with different names!
Visit PlanetMinecraft Page
GachaCraft Data Pack (Gacha/Loot Box System)
A short passion project of mine where I tried to overhaul how you play Minecraft; I integrated loot boxes or a gacha system into Minecraft. This features different "banners" or events that you can summon items from, and the more you pull, the better items you get through the pity system.
